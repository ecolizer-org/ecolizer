About Ecolizer Ventures LLP

Mission: To revive and restore the harmony of our environment through practical and sustainable solutions for environmental issues.

Vision: We aim to provide cost-effective and eco-friendly solutions to our customers for leading a better life by simultaneously boosting the local economy along with improving environmental indicators.

Since 2020, Ecolizer has been actively working with 22+ Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) in Odisha, ensuring end-to-end management of municipal solid waste, recovering 7000+ tons of plastic and 5000+ tons of other waste materials.
