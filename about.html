About Ecolizer Ventures LLP

Reviving environmental harmony through innovative waste management solutions since 2020.

Our Mission

To revive and restore the harmony of our environment through practical and sustainable solutions for environmental issues, focusing on waste management and recycling.

Our Vision

To provide cost-effective and eco-friendly solutions to our customers for leading a better life by simultaneously boosting the local economy and improving environmental indicators.

Our Journey

2020 - Foundation

Ecolizer Ventures LLP was established with a vision to revolutionize waste management in Odisha.

2021 - First Partnerships

Collaborated with Urban Local Bodies (ULBs) to implement end-to-end municipal solid waste management solutions.

2022-2024 - Growth & Impact

Expanded to 22+ ULBs, recovered 7000+ tons of plastic and 5000+ tons of other waste materials. Empowered SHG women and introduced IoT-based collection.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Committed to eco-friendly practices that benefit both people and the planet.

Community Empowerment

Empowering local communities, especially women, through skill development and entrepreneurship.

Innovation

Leveraging technology and innovative approaches to solve complex environmental challenges.
