The Challenge

Background

Cuttack, known as the Millennium City of Odisha, faced significant challenges in municipal solid waste management. The city generated over 300 tons of waste daily, with inefficient collection systems leading to:

  • Overflowing landfills and environmental pollution
  • High operational costs for the municipality
  • Limited material recovery and revenue generation
  • Health and sanitation issues for residents

Our Solution

Comprehensive Approach

Ecolizer implemented a holistic waste management solution focusing on wealth centre operations:

1. IoT-Based Collection System

Deployed smart sensors and GPS tracking for real-time monitoring of waste collection vehicles, optimizing routes and ensuring timely service.

2. Workforce Management

Empowered local SHG women through training programs, creating sustainable employment opportunities and improving operational efficiency.

3. Maximum Value Extraction

Established advanced sorting and processing facilities achieving 90% material recovery from dry waste.

Results & Impact

Operational Efficiency

Reduced collection time by 40% through optimized routes and real-time tracking, allowing for expanded service coverage.

Material Recovery

Achieved 90% recovery rate from municipal solid waste, recovering 7,000+ tons of plastic and other recyclables annually.

Economic Benefits

Generated significant revenue for the municipality through sale of recovered materials, reducing operational costs by 30%.

Community Impact

Created 500+ sustainable jobs for local women, improved public health, and enhanced environmental awareness in Cuttack.
